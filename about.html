About Pinnacle Shield Insurance

Pinnacle Shield Insurance was founded in 1985 in the heart of Hartford, Connecticut. For over four decades, we have been dedicated to providing reliable, innovative, and affordable insurance solutions to individuals and families across the United States.

With a commitment to integrity and customer service, our company has grown to serve customers in all 50 states. We offer a comprehensive range of insurance products, including auto, home, and life insurance, tailored to meet the unique needs of our diverse clientele.

Our team of experienced professionals is passionate about protecting what matters most to you. At Pinnacle Shield Insurance, we believe in building lasting relationships and delivering peace of mind through every policy we provide.
